INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY

The Information System (SI) becomes the "cornerstone" of any organization, because the information itself is one of the strategic resources of modern business.

The "Information System Security Policy (PSSI)" is a document that aims to put the strategic guidelines in black and white. the company with its computer resources (PC, Servers, Mail,…) according to the business requirements of the company.

Intervalle Technologies offers its clients a service to implement the "Information System Security Policy" and Charters using their computer resources in accordance with ISO 2700 X and the business needs of the environment in question.

RISK ANALYSIS

Intervalle Technologies offers to accompany you in your risk management approach based on the MEHARI methodology.

Our methodology is approved by the European Computer Security Agency under the Risk Management - Risk Assessment Tools category.

Our methodology is capable of assessing physical, logical and organizational security according to ISO 27001/BS 7799, as well as generate WSIS documentation for ISO 27001 certification.
