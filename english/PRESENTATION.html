About

Intervalle Technologies is a group of recognized experts and engineers who provide organizations with their expertise for their missions:

Audit of the security of computer networks, telecommunications and information systems. We perform audit missions that meet international requirements and standards, as well as more stringent audits that meet a specific need, such as intrusive testing, source code audits, or configuration;

Consulting that covers the design of security solutions, the study and formalization of needs, the development of security plans and security policies;

Information systems security training. Our training is designed to enable security officials and administrators companies to better understand the risks and threats faced by the systems they are responsible for, as well as the skills necessary to put into place solutions for protection;

Development of secure applications. We offer our clients (mainly offshore) development services secure applications such as transactional web applications or cryptographic mechanisms.
