Intervalle Technologies Partner and Accredited Training Center (ISC) ² for the MENA region is organizing the 1st CISSP training session in the BOOT CAMP format in Algeria.

The CISSP certification is the most famous and prestigious in the world in the field of Information Systems Security. It is also one of the most difficult to obtain.

The intensive BOOTCAMP formula offered by Intervalle Technologies with the support of (ISC) ² will allow participants to acquire all the theoretical and technical knowledge to pass and pass the exam certification CISSP .
  • Intensive Training for Exam Preparation CISSP
  • IMMERSION Total in an environment conducive to concentration in the enchanting setting of the Hotel "Le Caid Boussaada"
  • The Bootcamp is designed to push participants to go further than they would during classical training
  • Additional daily reviews are offered in BOOTCAMP throughout the day at the end of each module.
  • Official content of the updated CISSP training provided by an accredited instructor to (ISC) ²
  • Rich and varied program combining the official training seminar, workshops, brainstorming and expert intervention
